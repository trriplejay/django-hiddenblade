It's assassins time! come on, grab your friends.

Create a group, invite your friends, set some ground rules, and start a game.

For now, the only game type available is of the traditional "assassins" style, where each player is assigned a target, and killing their target yields a new one until the last assassin standing becomes the winner!

Future Features:

  1. image status update support
  2. text messaging to log your kills
  3. multiple game modes auto-moderated
  4. twitter integration for easy communication
