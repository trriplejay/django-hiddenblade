It's assassins time! come on, grab your friends.

Create a group, invite your friends, set some ground rules, and start a game.

At first, the only game type available is in the traditional "assassins" style, where each player is assigned a target, and killing their target yields a new one until the last assassin standing becomes the winner!

In the future we hope to make other game types available, such as free for all, team deathmatch, protect the VIP, and more!
