It's assassins time!

Create a group, invite your friends, set some ground rules, and start a game.

For now, the only game type available is of the traditional "assassins" style, where each player is assigned a target, and killing their target yields a new one until the last assassin standing becomes the winner!

Future Features:

  1. profile pictures
  2. image status update support
    • attach a kill image to your action
    • render image/video links in-line in the stream
  3. text messaging support
    • submit your kill (with photo)
    • receive updates/notifications
  4. multiple game modes auto-moderated
    • standard (done)
    • FFA
    • Team Deathmatch
    • Protect the VIP
    • Zombie Takeover
  5. twitter integration
  6. standalone mobile app with extra GPS fun?!
