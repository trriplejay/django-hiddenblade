Welcome to Hiddenblade!


A web page for managing and organizing Live Action Games, such as assassins.

You can start off by looking for a group to join. You'll need the moderator's permission before you can play. If you've already got some friends who are ready to play, create a group of your own!

If this is your first time on the site, your first step should be to create an account!

Though this website is designed to help moderate this style of game, it will still rely heavily on the honor system, so to ensure a good time is had by all, be honest and be excellent to each other!

See the about page for information on planned enhancements

Assassins (standard):

When starting a game, all players that are members of the group and are currently active are automatically added to the list of participants.

The list of participants is then randomized, and everyone is assigned a target. Not even the group admins can look at the assignments (that means they can play, too!)

When a player kills their target, they will receive a new target, which was their old target's target... target.

When there is only one player remaining, they are declared the winner and the game comes to an end.

Detailed game stats can be viewed after the game's completion, including who killed who in what order.

When an event occurs (game start, game end, player killed) the details of the event will be posted for the group to see. Be creative with your descriptions!
